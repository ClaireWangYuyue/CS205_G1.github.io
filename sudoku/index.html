What is Giant Sudoku Problem?

What is the problem you are trying to solve with this application?

  • A combinatorial number placement puzzle
  • The objective is to fill a 9×9 grid (board) with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3×3 subgrids (boxes) contains all of the digits from 1 to 9
  • Higher-rank sudokus have n2×n2 board with n×n boxes (n>3)
  • A well-posed puzzle has a unique solution
